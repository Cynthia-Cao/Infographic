18 Counties Withheld Funds from Domestic Violence Shelters in 2017

* Hover around the controller bar to track the distributed funds of each county
* Counties with undefined items did not submit a survey or answer the survey fully.
* The data of the City of Saint Louis is combined with the data of Saint Louis County.

By Chengzi Cao, Autumn Black, Pate McCuien, Regan Huston
Source: Missouri State Highway Patrol data

According to a Missouri statute, counties gather funds from marraige and court fees soecifically for the purpose of funding domestic violence shelters. Many Missouri counties distributed their funds successfully to shelters across the state. However, 18 counties did not distribute the funds to domestic violence shelters in 2017. Some counties say that the applications they are recieving are flawed, leaving them sitting with the money. However, Missouri State Auditor Nicole Galloway claims the application process itself is the issue.
